Newton Pizza's Secret Recipe

Description

The best pizza of all time is a handcrafted masterpiece, starting with a perfectly crispy yet chewy wood-fired crust, blistered in spots from the oven's intense heat. The base is brushed lightly with extra virgin olive oil, allowing the natural flavors of the dough to shine through. A delicate layer of San Marzano tomato sauce, both tangy and sweet, is spread evenly, offering a balance of acidity and sweetness. Topped with a mix of fresh mozzarella and aged provolone, the cheese melts into bubbly pockets of creamy richness, with hints of sharpness from the provolone. Generous slices of pepperoni, curled and crispy at the edges, bring a smoky, spicy bite. Fresh basil leaves are scattered on top, providing a fragrant herbal note.Finally, a drizzle of golden olive oil and a dusting of freshly grated Parmesan complete the pizza, elevating the flavors with richness and umami. Every bite is a harmonious blend of crispy, chewy, savory, and fresh, making it a timeless classic that satisfies every craving.

Ingredients

  • 5 blend cheese
  • Tandoori chicken
  • Hand tossed dough
  • Freshly grown cilantro
  • Freshly ground pizza sauce

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Add the sauce
  3. Layer the cheese
  4. Add toppings
  5. Bake and serve
