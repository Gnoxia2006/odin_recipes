SUBWAYYYYYYYYY

The Amazing and Bestest and Most Authenticest Subway Recipe

Description

A classic Subway sandwich is a custom-built, fresh sub that starts with your choice of soft, freshly baked bread. Inside, a variety of deli meats like turkey, ham, or salami are layered with crisp vegetables like lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, and onions, topped with cheese that melts perfectly if toasted. Drizzle your preferred sauces—mayo, mustard, or something spicy—over the sandwich for added flavor, and finish it off with a sprinkle of salt, pepper, or herbs. Each bite is a perfect blend of savory meats, crunchy veggies, creamy sauces, and soft bread.

Ingredients

  • Italian Herbs and Cheese Bread
  • Chipotle and Mayo Sauce
  • Olives
  • Onions
  • spinach
  • CHeddar cheese

Steps

  1. Choose your bread
  2. Add the meats
  3. Add the cheese
  4. Pile of the vegetables
  5. Add the sauces
  6. Finish with some salt ad pepper
